Traditional Marketing Agencies: What They Offer

Traditional agencies typically follow a standardized approach: strategy development, creative execution, media buying, and performance reporting. They rely heavily on human expertise and manual processes.

Typical Services:

  • • Creative design and content production
  • • Media planning and buying
  • • Social media management
  • • Performance reporting and analysis

Pricing Models & Timelines

How to Choose the Right Consultant

Essential Questions to Ask

1. What's your methodology for data analysis?

Look for AI-powered tools and real-time analytics capabilities.

2. Can you show me specific ROI improvements?

Ask for detailed case studies with measurable results.

3. How do you handle personalization at scale?

Ensure they can deliver individual-level targeting.

4. What technology stack do you use?

Verify they have access to cutting-edge AI tools.

Red Flags to Avoid

❌ Generic "one-size-fits-all" approaches

Every business is unique and requires customized strategies.

❌ No clear measurement framework

Results should be measurable and trackable.

❌ Promises unrealistic results

Be wary of consultants promising overnight success.

❌ Lack of industry experience

D2C marketing requires specific expertise and understanding.
