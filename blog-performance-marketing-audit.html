Campaign Structure Issues (Issues #1-5)

Issue #1: Poor Campaign Architecture

Critical

Symptoms to Look For:

  • • Ad groups containing 50+ keywords
  • • Mixing brand and generic terms in same campaigns
  • • No campaign structure logic or organization
  • • Single ad group targeting multiple intent types

Impact Analysis:

  • • 40% higher cost-per-click due to poor quality scores
  • • Reduced control over budget allocation
  • • Difficulty in performance optimization
  • • Poor ad relevance and user experience

Solution Framework:

  1. 1. Implement SKAG (Single Keyword Ad Groups) strategy
  2. 2. Separate campaigns by intent (brand, competitor, generic)
  3. 3. Create logical campaign hierarchy
  4. 4. Implement proper naming conventions

Issue #2: Inadequate Audience Targeting

Critical

Common Problems:

  • • Over-broad targeting without exclusions
  • • No lookalike or custom audience utilization
  • • Ignoring audience insights and behaviors
  • • Lack of audience layering and combinations

Performance Impact:

  • • 60% of traffic from irrelevant audience segments
  • • Higher cost-per-acquisition due to poor targeting
  • • Lower engagement rates and conversion quality
  • • Wasted budget on non-converting audiences

Issue #3: Landing Page Misalignment

Important

Red Flags:

  • • Generic landing pages for specific ad campaigns
  • • Message mismatch between ads and landing pages
  • • Poor mobile experience optimization
  • • No conversion tracking implementation

Business Impact:

  • • 70% higher bounce rates from misaligned traffic
  • • Significant conversion rate losses
  • • Poor quality score impacts
  • • Reduced ad platform algorithm performance

Tracking and Attribution Problems (Issues #6-10)

Issue #6: Incomplete Conversion Tracking

Missing or incorrectly configured conversion tracking leads to poor optimization decisions and wasted budget.

Impact: 25-40% budget waste

Issue #7: Cross-Platform Attribution Gaps

Inability to track customer journey across multiple touchpoints results in incomplete performance data.

Impact: 30-50% attribution loss

Issue #8: UTM Parameter Inconsistencies

Inconsistent UTM tagging makes it impossible to accurately measure campaign performance.

Impact: 20-35% data accuracy loss

Issue #9: Customer Journey Visibility

Lack of visibility into the complete customer journey prevents effective optimization.

Impact: 40-60% optimization opportunity loss

Issue #10: ROI Measurement Inaccuracies

Incorrect ROI calculations lead to poor budget allocation and strategic decisions.

Impact: 50-80% budget misallocation

Creative and Messaging Issues (Issues #11-15)

Issue #11: Ad Creative Fatigue

Declining performance over time due to lack of creative refresh strategy.

Impact: 30-50% performance decline

Issue #12: Weak Value Propositions

Generic messaging without clear differentiation or emotional connection.

Impact: 40-60% conversion loss

Issue #13: Poor Call-to-Action Strategy

Weak or confusing CTAs that don't drive desired actions.

Impact: 25-45% action rate loss

Issue #14: Missing Social Proof

Lack of testimonials, reviews, or trust signals in advertising.

Impact: 15-30% trust factor loss

Issue #15: Inadequate Testing

No systematic A/B testing program or poor testing methodology.

Impact: 20-40% optimization loss
