Pricing Red Flags and Green Flags

Avoid These Pricing Red Flags

Unusually Low Pricing

  • • ₹25K monthly for "comprehensive AI services"
  • • No clear deliverables or success metrics
  • • One-size-fits-all pricing without customization
  • • No proven case studies or results

Concerning Engagement Models

  • • Large upfront payments with no guarantees
  • • Vague service descriptions and deliverables
  • • No performance measurement or reporting
  • • Lack of transparent communication

Look for These Pricing Green Flags

Transparent Pricing Structure

  • • Clear service definitions and deliverables
  • • Detailed cost breakdown and justification
  • • Multiple engagement model options
  • • Pricing aligned with expected outcomes

Performance-Based Elements

  • • Success metrics and KPI definitions
  • • Performance guarantees or risk mitigation
  • • Milestone-based payment structures
  • • Results-tied bonus opportunities
