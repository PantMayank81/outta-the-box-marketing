Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

Outta The Box Marketing is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with Indian data protection laws.

Outta The Box Marketing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our AI-powered D2C marketing services.

This Privacy Policy complies with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and applicable international data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • → Fill out our contact forms or lead generation forms
  • → Request our free D2C growth assessment
  • → Subscribe to our newsletters or ACCELERATE Framework™ updates
  • → Contact us via email, phone, or LinkedIn
  • → Engage with our AI-powered marketing consultation services

1.2 Business Information

For D2C founders and businesses, we may collect:

  • → Company name and business details
  • → Revenue range (₹5-50Cr classification)
  • → Marketing challenges and objectives
  • → Current marketing performance metrics
  • → Industry and target market information

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect technical information including:

  • → IP address and browser type
  • → Operating system and device information
  • → Website usage patterns and analytics
  • → Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • → Referral sources and search terms used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • → Service Delivery: To provide AI-powered D2C marketing strategies and ACCELERATE Framework™ implementation
  • → Assessment & Consultation: To deliver personalized growth assessments and strategic recommendations
  • → Communication: To respond to inquiries and provide ongoing support for our 30-day money-back guarantee
  • → Performance Tracking: To measure and optimize our AI-driven marketing campaigns for 300% ROI delivery
  • → Legal Compliance: To comply with Indian legal obligations and maintain business records
  • → Service Improvement: To enhance our AI algorithms and strategic methodologies

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • → AI-powered marketing technology platforms
  • → Website analytics and optimization tools
  • → Email marketing and automation systems
  • → Payment processing for our services

3.2 Legal Requirements

When required by Indian law or legal process, including:

  • → Compliance with Information Technology Act, 2000
  • → Response to government authorities or court orders
  • → Protection of our legal rights and business interests
  • → Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of merger, acquisition, or business transfer, your information may be transferred to the new entity under equivalent privacy protections.

4. Data Security and Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • → Technical Safeguards: SSL encryption, secure servers, and firewall protection
  • → Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • → Data Monitoring: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • → Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, Indian-based servers with backup protocols

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to using reasonable security measures.

5. Your Rights Under Indian Law

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable regulations, you have the right to:

  • → Access: Request information about your personal data we hold
  • → Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • → Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • → Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • → Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • → Active Clients: During service period plus 7 years for business records
  • → Prospective Clients: Up to 3 years for follow-up and service improvement
  • → Marketing Communications: Until you unsubscribe or opt-out
  • → Legal Requirements: As required by Indian taxation and business laws

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • → Enhance your browsing experience and website functionality
  • → Analyze website traffic and user behavior patterns
  • → Deliver personalized content and marketing messages
  • → Optimize our AI-powered marketing recommendations

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit website functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

While we primarily store data in India, some information may be transferred internationally for:

  • → AI-powered marketing platform integration
  • → Cloud-based analytics and optimization tools
  • → Global client collaboration and reporting

We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards and contractual obligations.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for business professionals and D2C founders. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we discover we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
